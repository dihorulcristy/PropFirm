## Prop Firm Evaluation Impact

### HFT in Evaluations

**Best For**:
- Traders with scalping experience
- Those who can trade full-time
- Automated trading systems (EAs)
- Quick profit target achievement

**Challenges**:
- Daily loss limits (easy to hit with many trades)
- Spread costs eat into profits
- Requires firm to allow HFT/scalping
- Mental fatigue leads to mistakes

**Success Rate**: ~30-40% (high risk of overtrading)

### Swing Trading in Evaluations

**Best For**:
- Part-time traders
- Those with day jobs
- Patient, disciplined traders
- Trend-following strategies

**Challenges**:
- Slower to hit profit targets
- Overnight/weekend gaps
- Requires larger stop losses
- Fewer opportunities to recover from losses

**Success Rate**: ~50-60% (more controlled approach)

## Risk Management Comparison

### HFT Risk Profile
- **Risk per trade**: 0.1-0.5% (small)
- **Daily risk**: 2-5% (high due to volume)
- **Drawdown risk**: HIGH (many trades = more chances to fail)
- **Recovery difficulty**: HARD (need many wins to recover)

### Swing Trading Risk Profile
- **Risk per trade**: 1-2% (moderate)
- **Daily risk**: 1-2% (low due to fewer trades)
- **Drawdown risk**: MODERATE (fewer trades = fewer chances to fail)
- **Recovery difficulty**: MODERATE (one good trade can recover)

**Verdict**: Swing trading has better risk-adjusted returns!
