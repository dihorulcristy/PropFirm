## Tips for Beginners

### 1. Start Small
Begin with the smallest account size ($5K-$10K). It's cheaper to learn on a small account.

### 2. Practice First
Use demo accounts for at least 2-3 months before attempting challenges.

### 3. Learn Risk Management
Never risk more than 1-2% per trade. This is key to passing challenges.

### 4. Choose No Time Limit
As a beginner, avoid time pressure. Pick firms with no time limits.

### 5. Don't Overtrade
Quality over quantity. 1-2 solid trades per day is enough.
