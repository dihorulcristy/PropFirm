## First Payout vs Subsequent Payouts

### First Payout (Slower)
Most firms have longer processing for first payout:
- **Verification**: Identity, address, banking
- **Compliance**: AML/KYC checks
- **Setup**: Payment method configuration
- **Typical Time**: 2-4 weeks

### Subsequent Payouts (Faster)
Once verified, payouts are much faster:
- **No Verification**: Already on file
- **Automated**: System processes automatically
- **Typical Time**: 1-14 days (firm dependent)

**Pro Tip**: Get your first payout ASAP to speed up future payouts!

## Payout Strategy Tips

### 1. Reinvest Some Profits
**Strategy**:
- Take 50% as payout
- Leave 50% in account
- Compound your growth

**Example**:
```
Month 1 Profit: $2,000
Payout: $1,000
Reinvest: $1,000
New Balance: $101,000

Month 2 Profit: $2,100 (on larger balance)
Payout: $1,050
Reinvest: $1,050
New Balance: $102,050
```

### 2. Regular Small Payouts vs Large Lump Sums
**Small Regular**:
- Better cash flow
- Lower risk
- Easier budgeting

**Large Lump Sums**:
- Fewer transactions
- May reduce fees
- Good for big purchases

### 3. Diversify Across Firms
**Strategy**:
- Trade with 2-3 firms
- Stagger payout schedules
- Ensure consistent income

**Example**:
```
Firm A: Payouts 1st and 15th
Firm B: Payouts weekly (Fridays)
Firm C: On-demand

Result: Income every week!
```
