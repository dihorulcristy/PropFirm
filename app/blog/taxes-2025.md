## Quarterly Estimated Taxes (US)

### When to Pay

**Deadlines**:
- Q1 (Jan-Mar): April 15
- Q2 (Apr-May): June 15
- Q3 (Jun-Aug): September 15
- Q4 (Sep-Dec): January 15 (next year)

### How Much to Pay

**Safe Harbor Rule**:
- Pay 100% of last year's tax (110% if income > $150k)
- OR pay 90% of current year's estimated tax

**Example Calculation**:
```
Expected Annual Profit: $100,000
Estimated Tax Rate: 40% (federal + self-employment + state)
Annual Tax: $40,000
Quarterly Payment: $10,000
```

**Pro Tip**: Set aside 40-50% of every payout for taxes!
